Privacy Policy

At Kartoffel Music, we take your privacy very seriously. Our service offers a seamless experience by enabling you to download your music directly from the cloud onto your device. For this, we may store your data and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our products and services.

Kartoffel Music's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

What Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we may request certain cloud login information such as your name, email address, and other contact details. This information is collected when you connect to Cloud in our service. We enable you to download from Cloud, eg. Google Drive, and we do store your files in your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to provide you a comfortable offline music play service. This includes allowing you to log in using your Google account and facilitating the download and storage of data from your Google Drive. Once downloaded, you can access your music collection even when you're offline, eliminating the need for a stable internet connection.

How We Protect Your Information

Your personal information is stored only on your device and we do not provide a way to export your information outside of your device. That being said, we do not keep your information outside of your device. While we take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, it is important for you to also take precautions to protect your data. Ensure that you use strong, unique passwords on your device and keep them confidential.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by an announcement on our app.
